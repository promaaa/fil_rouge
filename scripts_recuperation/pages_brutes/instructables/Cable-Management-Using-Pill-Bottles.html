See my instructable/video to remove labels from pill bottles. After you remove the label, we are ready to start.

Needless to say, you should use larger bottles for cable management.

Step 1: You Will Need a Drill and Bit

I just used a wood bore speed bit. 3/4 inch. You will use it to make a hole in the bottle cap.

Step 2: Pull the Cable Through the Cap

Find about the center of cable and pull it through leaving about the length you will need on the other side.

Step 3: Gently Fold the Cable

Gently fold the cable to about the length of the pill bottle.

Step 4: Place the Folded Cable Into the Pill Bottle

And then screw on the cap.

Step 5: Enjoy

Shortening the cable, ready for travel if need be.

Step 6: Computer Speaker Wire Before

Messy spaghetti laying on the carpet.

Step 7: Just Fold and Insert Into Pill Bottle

No more tripping hazard.
