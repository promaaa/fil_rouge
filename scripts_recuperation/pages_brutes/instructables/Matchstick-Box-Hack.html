This is a simple hack made with just an empty matchstick box. It is also very useful and will help by making it easier to light a matchstick...

Supplies

An empty Matchstick box

A Scissor

Step 1: Shaping the Cover

First, cut out the backside of the cover and fold the sides inwards as shown in the images above.

Step 2: Shaping the Inner Box

Cut all the outer edges like in the above image and fold the longer sides inwards as shown in the 2nd image.

Step 3: Final Step

Now follow the images above:

=>Insert one of the shorter edge of inner box in the folds of the cover

=>Insert the second edge in the other side (this might be tricky)

Now it will look like the 3rd image attached.

Step 4: Working

Insert some matchsticks as shown in the image above, hold the lower side of the cover and quickly pull out the matchstick as shown in the video attached below.
