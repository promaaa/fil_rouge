This is an easy way to fold shirts where you can see the logo. I hope you enjoy!

Step 1: Fold Arms

Flip your shirt so the logo is facing the ground. Fold the arms back onto the shirt.

Step 2: Fold the Halves

Fold each half of the shirt to the middle

Step 3: Final Fold

Fold the bottom edge of the shirt to the middle and repeat.

Step 4: In the Drawer

You can stack or layer your shirts to create an organized, easy-to-see assembly of shirts.
