Tinkercad Projects »

Heres how to make a toilet paper holder

Step 1: How to Create

go to https://www.tinkercad.com/ and create a new project

Step 2:

once you are in your project select the circle from basic shapes.

Step 3:

go to the polygon shape and make it a hole, put it in the circle, and hit enter.

Step 4:

select stick from the everyday objects tab.

Step 5:

rotate the stick the desirable position(as seen in photo)

Step 6:

make stick red and duplicate it.

Step 7:

rotate circle by 90 degrees

Step 8:

create small rectangular object.

Step 9:

place the rectangular object on top of the sticks.

Step 10:

duplicate the object by pressing shift and clicking each thing then hit the duplicate button on the top left.

Step 11:

creat polygon then rotate it to 90 degrees.

Step 12:

make it 90mm long.

Step 13:

using shift select the 3 objects on the right and merge them into one.

Step 14:

use hold shift again to select all the rest of them and hit merge.

Step 15:

done!
