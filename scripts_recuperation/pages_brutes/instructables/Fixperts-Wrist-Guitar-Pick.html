Fixperts - Wrist Guitar Pick

Introduction: Fixperts - Wrist Guitar Pick

As part of an educational project - FIXPERTS, at the Holon Institute of Technology - HIT, in Israel.


We designed the wrist guitar pick for Ariel, Who diagnosed with CP that effect his right side of the body and the fine motor skills in his right hand .

Help him play the guitar as he like, fast or slow and up or down the strings!


We designed 2 versions, one based on 3D printing only, the other for those who have access to orthoplast (a material used to make splints)


You are welcome to download and prepare yourself!

Supplies

For version 1:

22 mm wide polyester strip (25 cm long)

velcro strip (5 cm long)



For version 2:

22 mm wide polyester strip (25 cm long)

velcro strip (5 cm long)

bias ribbon (50 cm long)

wax string (10 cm long)
