Sunboard-- Cardboard Sunvisor for Eyeglasses

Introduction: Sunboard-- Cardboard Sunvisor for Eyeglasses

Living an active outdoor life means being in the blaring sun. With a piece cardboard, a ball cap and scissors you can make a simple comfortable eyeglass friendly visor. It won't muss your hairdo like a ball cap or give you a headache like some visors.

Supplies

Cardboard
Marking Pens
Ball cap
Scissors
Clothes pins

Step 1: Trace Visor

Use ball cap to trace out visor. Clothes pins will help get to the forehead area.

Step 2: Finish Layout, Cut and Form

layout a 1/2 inch by 1 inch square at temple of visor. Cut out visor and at dots cut out square for eyeglass temples, form into a sunblocking curve.

Step 3: Put on Glasses and Chill in the Sun.

Slip eyeglass temple though visor temples place over eyes and adjust for comfort. This visor is easily made and cool and comfortable to wear. It can be blinged-up with felt cloth and shiny things or prettied-up with color pens.
