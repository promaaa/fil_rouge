Handy Phone Holder

Have you ever used a phone stand just to find out that it doesn't hold your phone properly? Or do they just look boring? For me this happens a lot and I'm annoyed at low quality phone holders - they make the overall experience unsatisfactory. I did some research on existing phone stands that are affordable ($20 or below) and they either are made of cheap plastic with bad finishes or are not stable enough. So I designed a phone stand that looks and works better, and is VERY easy to make. NO GLUING NEEDED

You don't need to have access to a CNC router for this - a 3D printer works as well!

I will be highlighting my design process as it is just as simple as sending the model to your CNC / 3D Printer.
