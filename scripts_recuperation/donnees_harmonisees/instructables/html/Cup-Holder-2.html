Cup Holder

A 3D-printed cup holder designed specifically for hot cups. As tea lovers and coffee enthusiasts know, enjoying a hot beverage is not just about the drink itself but the experience that accompanies it. Our cup holder enhances this experience by providing a safe, stylish, and functional solution for holding hot cups without the risk of burns or spills.

Crafted using advanced 3D printing technology, this cup holder is lightweight yet robust, making it perfect for everyday use. Its ergonomic design ensures a comfortable grip while securely holding cups of various sizes. The heat-resistant materials used in its construction protect your hands from high temperatures, allowing you to savor your favorite drinks without discomfort.
