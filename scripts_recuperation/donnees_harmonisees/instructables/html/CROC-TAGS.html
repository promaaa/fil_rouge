Problem:

My Crocs footwear has become very common in my school, and often times it is hard to know my particular footwear. I came up with this solution to identify my footwear in public and also to make sure that I don't mix it up when I go into a room with my friends.

The initials "F" and "U" are after my roommate's initials, Frederick Uka, who always loves to wear Crocs once he gets the chance.

i used tinkercad for the letters, because its a lot easier, then transitioned fusion 360 for the clip,which is a lot easier to achieve with fusion 360 tools

      • Using the overall slot tool, sketch a slot "12mm" long and "2.5mm" wide.

      • Create a sketch on the top face of the extruded slot and offset the slot by "2mm".
      • Extrude the offset sketch by "2mm".
      • Fillet the top face of the extruded sketch by "2mm".
