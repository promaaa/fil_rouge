Keep Your Shoes Tied Hack

How many times do your shoes come untied in a day? Keep your shoes tied ALL day with this simple hack. Bonus: No Annoying Double-Knot to pick at the end of the day.

Seriously, no more retying during the big game; no more retying running to catch your ride; no more retying in the hall at work or school. (No more retying your kids shoes.) One simple tie, and your shoes stay tied all day. And, when you are ready to take your shoes off, untying is simple - no more knot picking.
