One-handed Vegetable Peeler

Our client Aaron needed a way to peel vegetables safely and efficiently while using one hand. He has a limb difference and does many things one-handed, including cooking. There are safety concerns with how he currently peels and it is time consuming. Our team took on the challenge to design and build a simple solution to allow Aaron to peel vegetables so he can enjoy cooking again.
