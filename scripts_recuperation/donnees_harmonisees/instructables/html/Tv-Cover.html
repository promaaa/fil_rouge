Tv Cover

I like to listen to youtube/music before bed on my tv but sometimes it gets much too bright depending on what i'm listening to. Even after turning the brightness all the way down I still can't sleep. So my idea is to make clips that you can attach a curtain to. You can put this in front of your tv so little brightness comes straight on.

Lien du tutoriel de base : https://www.instructables.com/Tv-Cover/
