HOW TO DO NOTHING

Many people have asked me, Medha, what do you do in your free time? 10% of the time, I respond with "Nothing". But how can I do literally nothing? There has to be something that I am doing. No. I do nothing. But it isn't as easy as sitting around, staring at paint dry. It is an art that not anyone can understand, or has the determination to.

It had all started with when I was in elementary school. On a Summer Saturday evening, I had just turned on the fan to drown out the heat with the cool breeze generated from it, but something felt... off. There was nothing to do. But I couldn't just do literally nothing, could I? So I did everything I could to entertain myself, but it didn't help. That was until.... I had an epiphany. How about I just do... nothing!

It had taken me years to perfect but there is a formula to get the optimal experience out of doing nothing. It was through this technique that many countless days were passed without a problem.
