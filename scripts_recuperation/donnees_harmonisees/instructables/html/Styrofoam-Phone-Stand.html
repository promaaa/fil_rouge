Styrofoam Phone Stand

I made a styrofoam phone stand to have in your house and use when needed to hold up your phone and store other items like your headphones or chargers. This can be useful when watching a movie and not wanting to hold your phone yourself. I decided to use styrofoam because there have been many times when I get packages and just throw out the styrofoam, but I thought of a way to repurpose it.
