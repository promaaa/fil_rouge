Nest Thermostat - Setting the Schedule for Under 1 Hour

For ages I have been trying to set my Nest Thermostat to come on for a period less than 1 hour. Due to massive increases here in the UK in Gas and Eletric I didnt want the heating on for any longer than it needed to. So I found that I could use Alexa Routines to control my heating instead of the default scheduler.
