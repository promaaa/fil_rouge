How to Properly Fold a Fitted Sheet

Introduction: How to Properly Fold a Fitted Sheet

I am OCD and have always disliked not having a properly folded fitted sheet. I finally took matters into my own hands and did some research. I found many ways to properly fold a fitted sheet so the edges are smooth, and this technique is my favorite.

Supplies

One fitted sheet

Step 1:

Step 1: Spread out fitted sheet so all 4 corners are visible

Step 2:

Step 2: Take corner A into one hand, take corner B into other hand

Step 3:

Step 3: Turn corner B inside out

Step 4:

Step 4: Tuck corner B into Corner A. Corner A will be on the outside. Line up the seams of Corner A and Corner B.

Step 5:

Step 5: Take Corner A (Corner B is still inside) in one and and Corner C in other hand.

Step 6:

Step 6: Tuck Corner C into Corner B, keeping Corner A on the outside

Step 7:

Step 7: Take Corner A (Corner B and C are still tucked inside) in one hand and Corner D in other hand. Tuck Corner D into Corner C, keeping Corner A on the outside.

Step 8:

Step 8: Place sheet on flat surface with Corner A at the top and new Corner E at the bottom. It should look like a right triangle.

Step 9:

Step 9: Fold Corner E over Corner A

Step 10:

Step 10: Fold large end to small end until the tail is gone. You now have a folded fitted sheet!
