Introduction: Spa Towel Foldinfg

Here you will find step-by-step instructions to fold your towels just like they do at the spa! Not only are these cute, they also save room and stay nice and snug until you are ready to use them! I have included pictures for each step for towels and wash cloths so your bathroom can give all the spa vibes!

Supplies

  • Any size towel or wash cloth
  • Large surface (table, bed, floor, etc.)

Step 1: Spread Your Towel or Wash Cloth

Spread your towel across the surface you are working on. Make sure it is as flat as you can get it!

Step 2: First Fold

Fold the top right corner down leaving space on the left and bottom edge. (You will have a big space on the bottom if using a large towel as pictured.)

Step 3: Fold Again!

Take the right side and fold it as you would to fold the towel/wash cloth in half keeping your previous fold intact.

Step 4: Roll!

Starting at the bottom of the towel/washcloth, start rolling it upwards.

Step 5: Flip It!

Keep all folds in place and flip the towel/wash cloth to the opposite side. Your more triangular end should now be on the top right and you should not see any flaps.

Step 6: Triangle Flap

Stop rolling when there is a small triangular flap on the top right of your rolled towel/wash cloth. This will create a fold section on the right of your towel or wash cloth.

Step 7: Tuck!

Hold the towel/wash cloth in place with one hand and use the other to snuggly tuck the triangular flap into the fold section on the right side.

Step 8: Enjoy!

You now have a towel so cute it belongs at the spa!
